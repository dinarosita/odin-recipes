Tteokbokki

Description

Tteokbokki is a very popular rice cake (tteok) dish from Korea. I got to be aware of this dish after watching a bunch of Korean dramas.

Here is my own twist, combining recipe I followed with bunch of fun toppings I like, with reduced amount of the tteok itself.

Ingredients

  • Tteok (Korean rice cake), short tubular shape
  • 5-10 large anchovy
  • 5-10 pieces of kelp - well, depends how big they are
  • 3 tbs of gochujang (Korean fermented chili paste. Mildest)
  • 3 green onion, cut 2 cm long
  • 1-2 cup of water
  • 2 hard boiled eggs, peel
  • 2 sheets of fish cake
  • 1 sachet of sweetener
  • Optional

    Cut into bite size:

    • Enoki or other mushroom
    • Fish or beef balls
    • Fried/layered tofu
    • Konyaku

Steps

  1. Make broth: boil anchovy and kelp in 1-2 cup of water for 10-15 minutes, remove chunks from the pan.
  2. Boil frozen tteok just to soften
  3. Add gochujang and sweetener/sugar to the broth, mix well
  4. Add the rest of ingredients, cook a little more until everything is heated up.
