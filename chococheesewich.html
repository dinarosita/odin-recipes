Chococheesewich

Description

Chococheesewich is chocolate cheese sandwich, my husband's enemy. It's a very easy sweet snack. I likve to have it toasted or untoasted. In regular bread slices, in hotdog or burger buns - choose the flimsy kind though. I think it will be so good toasted in a sandwich press, but we don't own one.

Ingredients

  • Bread slices or bun
  • Butter
  • Chocolate sprinkles, but spread works too
  • Cheese slices, or shreeded is fine too

Steps

  1. Optional: toast the bread. Or if using sandwich press, assemble the content first.
  2. Add butter in chocolate srinkles side. Omit if using spread.
  3. Add cheese in. That's it.
